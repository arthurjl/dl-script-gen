Dataset Creation

The fun of this project comes from being able to style both the text generation and speech synthesis to people that we were familiar with. So, we collected this data online from documentaries, Youtube, and online courseware. Sometimes, it was difficult to ensure that the transcripts of the videos we found were high quality, this was especially true when we used auto-generated English captions from Youtube vieos.
